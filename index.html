After a certain amount of time, an original work protected by US copyright becomes free to use, without the original author's consent. There are great databases of public-domain work out there, but if you can't find what you're looking for there, answer the following questions!

Published is a term of art here. Generally, it means that you've shared the work outside of a normal group of friends and family for purposes of selling the work, licensing it, or otherwise distributing it.
